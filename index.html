Welcome to Open Question! If this is your first time here, head on over to the intro post. This is an ad-free site, and I hope to be able to keep it that way as long as possible. If you're a regular reader and enjoy our content, please consider donating so we never have to resort to ads to keep things going.

There are two main sections to this site: a blog and a collection of tech tutorials.

The blog will cover a number of subjects, but will primarily focus on critical thinking and scientific principles applied to things like developing a personal knowledge base, creating informed opinions on contemporary issues, and crafting decisions to positively influence the long-term development of humanity.

The tech tutorials will be a collection of introductions, tips, methodologies, and best practices for everything from software development and web publishing to data analysis and system maintenance. It will cover some of my favorite and most used tools as well as what I've found to be effective workflows for the way I do things.

This is, no doubt, quite the ecclectic mix of topics, so feel free to just stick with the area you're interested in, whether it's the blog or tutorials. I would encourage you, however, to give both sections a try. You might find something interesting!
